Organic First Grocery

Finding organic and wholesome foods can be challenging in Costa Rica. It is especially important because of the prolific use of Round-up and other harmful pesticides, banned in many countries. That is why we go out of our way to source products from certified organic farmers and food producers, and where this is not possible, from producers that have been committed to not using pesticides.

Organic Restaurant

Lead by our experienced chef Alonso, from Guanacaste, our fully licensed restaurant offers a wide selection of casual fare, pizzas with organic kamut or fresh organic yuca crust, burgers, sandwiches, vegan delights, seafood, and organic-fed, free-range chicken. We offer a selection of organic wine, artisan beer and hard kombucha on tap. 

Hot Drinks plus Superfood Elixirs

We offer a wide selection of hot beverages including locally roasted organic coffee, a selection of certified organic teas, and Anima Mundi elixirs and drink boosters, loaded with adaptogenic plant medicines, such as the Mushroom Mocha, Golden Sun Milk, Golden Moon Milk and Dirty Rose Chai. Sweetened with organic honey or stevia, and served with organic coconut milk, organic soy milk or fresh homemade organic hemp milk or almond milk.

Community EvENTS

Kapi Kapi is pleased to offer Community Open Mic and Karaoke every Saturday evening starting at 6pm. Our Karaoke app has over 45,000 songs to choose from and allows for duets. Jams, improvs, slam poets, comedy skits and more are welcome, so come express your talents. Bring your dancing shoes, as the vibe is sometimes so contagious you will want to get your groove on. 

In addition we offer periodic concerts from exceptional musicians from Costa Rica and around the world.

Who We Are

 

Michael Gallant has managed a community grocery store, a small chain of fair-trade cafes, and has been dedicated to holistic lifestyle for a quarter century. He manages the general business operations and you will find him roaming the aisles in the store or pulling shots on the Rancilio.��

Lazarina Todorova has been video documenting alternative and healthy lifestyles, including in her award winning documentary “Life After Babylon”, and her work at the Hippocrates Health Institute in Florida. Through her healing journey in overcoming Lyme disease, she has gained first hand knowledge about the importance of a healthy lifestyle, micronutrients, herbology and alternative therapies. Lazarina manages purchasing, events and marketing.

Based, in Vancouver, Canada, Andrew Rezmer, is a serial social entreprenuer and owner of the successful organic grocery store and vegan cafe, Eternal Abundance, in Vancouver, Canada. He has been working and playing in the holistic health, wellness and plant medicine space for many decades.
